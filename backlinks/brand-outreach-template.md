# Brand Outreach Template

> **Active template (2026-06-30):** Value-first outreach sequence.
> See `docs/monetization/brand-outreach-crm.md` for the full CRM and all templates.

## Quick Reference

The first email must NOT:
- Ask for a backlink
- Ask to be added to an "As Seen On" page
- Sell a paid package immediately
- Claim large traffic
- Imply guaranteed sales

The first email SHOULD:
- Name the product
- Give 3 concrete observations or buyer questions (based on our actual research)
- Point to the existing SmartPetGuide mention if relevant
- Ask whether a short visibility snapshot would be useful

## Active Sequence

### D0 Opener
```
Subject: 3 things buyers are asking about [Product Name]

Hi [Brand] team,

I run SmartPetGuide, an independent research site covering smart pet devices. We've been analyzing [Product Name] and noticed a few things in buyer reviews and search data that might be useful:

1. [Finding 1 — specific, data-backed observation from reviews or search]
2. [Finding 2]
3. [Finding 3]

No pitch — just thought you'd want to know. We see these patterns across a lot of products in [category].

If a quick visibility snapshot would be useful (showing how your product appears in Google, AI search, and review patterns), happy to put one together at no cost.

Best,
SmartPetGuide Research Team
press@smartpetguide.net
```

### D3 Follow-up
```
Subject: Re: [Product Name] visibility

Hi [Brand],

Following up — inboxes get busy. The free visibility snapshot offer stands. Zero cost, takes about 30 minutes. Covers Google visibility, AI citation readiness, and top buyer questions from your reviews.

Let me know if you'd like me to go ahead.
```

### D7 Close-loop
```
Subject: Re: [Product Name] — right contact?

Hi [Brand],

One last note — if marketing/partnerships isn't the right contact for this kind of research, could you point me to the right person? Otherwise I'll assume timing isn't right. Thanks for your time.
```

## Historical Template — Do Not Use

The template below asked brands for backlinks and press coverage placements. It produced **one eufy support-ticket reply and no useful partnerships.** Preserved for reference only.

```
Subject: Featured your product on SmartPetGuide — press coverage?

Hi [Brand Name] team,

I run SmartPetGuide (smartpetguide.net), an independent review site covering smart pet devices. We recently featured your [Product Name] in our [review/comparison/best list]:

[Link to our page featuring their product]

Our analysis is based on verified owner reviews and marketplace data — we've evaluated 26 products across 6 categories.

Would you consider adding us to your press coverage or "As Seen On" page? Happy to provide a logo or any assets you need.

Best,
SmartPetGuide Team
hello@smartpetguide.net
```

### Why it failed
- Asked for a favor (backlink) before giving any value
- "Press coverage" ask implies we're media — we're a research site
- No specific, brand-relevant insight about their product
- Contacted Petlibro, PETKIT, Catit — zero replies
