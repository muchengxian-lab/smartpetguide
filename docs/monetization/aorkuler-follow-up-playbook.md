# Aorkuler Follow-Up Playbook

> Context: John from Aorkuler replied on 2026-07-02 and requested the free visibility snapshot. PDF was sent the same day. Default to email-first, written follow-up. Do not push a live call unless John asks for one.

## Goal

Move from "thanks for the snapshot" to one of these concrete next steps:

1. Aorkuler confirms the snapshot is useful and asks for more detail.
2. Aorkuler shares which listing/content problem matters most to them.
3. Aorkuler requests a deeper written audit or scoped proposal.

Do not rush into a paid ask in the next reply unless John explicitly asks about pricing or scope.

## Response Rules

- Keep replies short: 120-180 words unless answering a detailed question.
- Always answer in writing first.
- Ask one question at the end, not three.
- Make the next step easy to forward internally.
- Avoid mentioning weak spoken English. If a call comes up, frame written notes as "easier to share internally."
- Preserve editorial boundaries: no paid ranking, no guaranteed positive coverage, no fabricated data.

SmartPetGuide Research Team
