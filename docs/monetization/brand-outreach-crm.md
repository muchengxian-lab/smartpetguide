## Outreach Sequence (one brand, one thread)

### D0: Opener — 3 Buyer Questions
**Goal:** Show we've actually looked at their product. Give value first. No ask.

Template:
```
Subject: 3 things buyers are asking about [Product Name]

Hi [Brand] team,

I run SmartPetGuide, an independent research site covering smart pet devices. We've been analyzing [Product Name] and noticed a few things in buyer reviews and search data that might be useful:

1. [Finding 1 — specific, data-backed]
2. [Finding 2]
3. [Finding 3]

No pitch — just thought you'd want to know. We see these patterns across a lot of products in [category].

If a quick visibility snapshot would be useful (showing how your product appears in Google, AI search, and review patterns), happy to put one together at no cost.

Best,
SmartPetGuide Research Team
press@smartpetguide.net
```

### D3: Follow-up — Mini Visibility Snapshot Offer
**Goal:** Remind. Offer the free snapshot again.

Template:
```
Subject: Re: [Product Name] visibility

Hi [Brand],

Following up on my note from Tuesday — I know inboxes get busy.

The offer stands: I can put together a free visibility snapshot for [Product Name] showing where your product shows up (or doesn't) in Google and AI search results, plus the top buyer questions hiding in your reviews. Takes me about 30 minutes, zero cost to you.

Let me know if you'd like me to go ahead.

Best,
SmartPetGuide Research Team
```

### D7: Close-loop
**Goal:** Confirm contact. Ask if they're the right person.

Template:
```
Subject: Re: [Product Name] — right contact?

Hi [Brand],

One last note — if marketing/partnerships isn't the right contact for this kind of research, could you point me to the right person?

Otherwise, I'll assume the timing isn't right and won't clutter your inbox further.

Best,
SmartPetGuide Research Team
```

## Reply Templates

### "Send me details"
```
Great — I'll put together a free visibility snapshot for [Product Name] and send it over within 2-3 business days. It'll cover:
- Where your product appears in Google search vs competitors
- Whether AI search engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity) cite accurate info about it
- The top 3 buyer questions or objections in your Amazon reviews

Anything specific you'd like me to focus on?
```

### "We only do affiliate"
```
Thanks for letting me know. We do participate in Amazon Associates — if there's an affiliate program or commission structure you work with, happy to discuss. In the meantime, the free visibility snapshot offer still stands if you'd find it useful.
```

### "Not interested"
```
Understood — thanks for the quick reply. If anything changes down the road, feel free to reach out anytime.
```

## Status Key

| Status | Meaning |
|------|------|
| Not contacted | In queue, ready for human to send |
| Sent — awaiting reply | D0 sent, no response yet |
| Replied — engaging | Brand responded positively |
| Replied — not interested | Brand declined |
| Free snapshot delivered | Snapshot sent, awaiting feedback |
| Audit discussion | Moving toward paid audit |
| Closed — no response | D7 sent, no reply |
